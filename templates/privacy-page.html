This Privacy Policy describes how Ellura Collections collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services. It also explains the choices you have regarding how your information is collected and used. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Information We Collect

  1. Personal Information. We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us on the website. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, or other contact information that allows us to identify and communicate with you.
  2. Account Information. If you create an account on our website, we may collect information such as your username, password, account preferences, purchase history, and other information associated with your account. This information allows us to manage your account and provide personalized services.
  3. Payment Information. We may collect payment-related information such as billing details and transaction records during your purchase or transaction. Payment information may be processed by third-party payment processors, and we do not store full credit information on our servers.
  4. Device Data. We may automatically collect certain technical data about the device you use to access our website. This may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and general location information. This data helps improve the website’s functionality and security.
  5. Cookies and tracking data. Ellura Collection may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help remember your preferences, understand how visitors use our website, and improve overall website performance.

How We Use Your Information

  1. To Provide and Maintain the Service. We use the information collected to maintain, operate and provide services offered on the website. This includes enabling user accounts, delivering requested services, and ensuring that the platform functions properly.
  2. To Improve the Website. We may use the collected data to analyze how users interact with the website. This helps us understand usage patterns, identify areas for improvement, and enhance overall user experience.
  3. To Communicate with Users. We may use your contact information to communicate with you regarding updates, service-related announcements, responses, and other relevant information about your account or our services.
  4. To Process Payments. Data about transactions may be used to process payments, confirm transactions, and manage billing activities. Payment processing may be handled securely by trusted third-party payment providers to ensure secure and efficient payment processing.
  5. To Comply with Legal Obligations. We may use collected information when required to comply with applicable laws, legal processes, government requests, or to enforce our terms, protect our rights, and maintain the security of our services.

How we share and disclose information

  1. Service Providers. We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our website and deliver our services. These providers may assist with tasks such as hosting, customer support, email delivery, data analysis, and website maintenance. They are only given access to the information necessary to perform their services and are required to protect your data.
  2. Payment Processors. If you make purchases or financial transactions through our website, your payment information may be shared with secure third-party payment processors that handle payment authorization and processing. These providers process your information according to their own privacy and security policies.
  3. Analytics Platforms. We may share certain usage information with analytics providers that help us understand how visitors interact with our website. These services collect data such as page visits, time spent on pages, and user behavior to help us improve our website and user experience. For example, we may use tools like Google Analytics to analyze website traffic.
  4. Legal Authorities. We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests from public authorities, such as a court order, subpoena, or government investigation. This may also occur when necessary to protect our rights, enforce our policies, or ensure the safety and security of our users and services.

Data Security and Protection

  1. Encryption. Ellura Collection uses appropriate encryption technologies to help protect sensitive information transmitted through our website or application. Encryption helps ensure that data such as personal details or login information is securely transferred and cannot easily be accessed by unauthorized parties during transmission.
  2. Secure Servers. Your information may be stored on secure servers that are protected using industry-standard security measures. These measures may include firewalls, monitoring systems, and restricted infrastructure designed to safeguard data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
  3. Limited Access. Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel, employees, or service providers who require the data to perform their duties. These individuals are required to handle your information responsibly and maintain its confidentiality.
  4. Security Monitoring and Updates. We regularly review and update our security practices to help protect against unauthorized access, data breaches, or misuse of information. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

User Rights

  1. Access Your Data. You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you. Upon request, we can provide details about the data we collect, how it is used, and who it may be shared with.
  2. Request Correction. If you believe that any personal information we hold about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request that we correct or update the information to ensure its accuracy.
  3. Request Deletion. You may request that we delete or remove your personal information from our systems, subject to certain legal or operational obligations that may require us to retain some data for a limited period.
  4. Opt Out of Marketing Communications. You have the right to opt out of receiving promotional or marketing communications from us at any time. You can do this by following the unsubscribe instructions included in our emails or by contacting us directly.
  5. Withdraw Consent. In situations where we rely on your consent to process your personal information, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. This will not affect the lawfulness of any processing carried out before the consent was withdrawn.
